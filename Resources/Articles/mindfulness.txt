Mindfulness is about being present in the moment. Even 5 minutes of deep breathing or meditation can reduce stress and improve your mental clarity throughout the day.

You don't need any special equipment or apps. Simply sit comfortably, close your eyes, and focus on your breath. When your mind wanders, gently bring it back to your breathing. That's it.

Practice makes progress. Like any skill, mindfulness gets easier with practice. Start with just 2-3 minutes a day and gradually increase as it becomes more natural.
